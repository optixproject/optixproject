About OPTIX

OPTIX is a transformative project aimed at creating sustainable Positive-Energy Districts (PEDs) by integrating renewable energy systems with cutting-edge digital platforms for real-time monitoring, optimization, and energy trading.

Aligned with the European Green Deal, OPTIX contributes to achieving climate neutrality by 2050 through innovative and scalable solutions.

Work Packages

OPTIX is structured into several key work packages to ensure effective project implementation:

  • WP1: Project Coordination and Management
  • WP2: Technology Integration for PEDs
  • WP3: Digital Twin Development
  • WP4: Energy Trading and Blockchain Solutions
  • WP5: Pilot Demonstrations
  • WP6: Knowledge Dissemination
