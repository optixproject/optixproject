Welcome to OPTIX

Optimising Positive-Energy Districts through Interoperable Digital Platforms

About OPTIX

OPTIX is a transformative project aimed at creating sustainable Positive-Energy Districts (PEDs) by integrating renewable energy systems with cutting-edge digital platforms for real-time monitoring, optimization, and energy trading.

Aligned with the European Green Deal, OPTIX contributes to achieving climate neutrality by 2050 through innovative and scalable solutions.

Our Objectives

  • Develop and validate advanced renewable energy solutions, including solar PV and innovative storage systems.
  • Create and test state-of-the-art digital twins and blockchain-based energy trading platforms.
  • Demonstrate the feasibility of PEDs in diverse urban and climate contexts through pilot programs.
  • Provide a scalable framework to guide future PED implementations worldwide.

Pilot Demonstrations

OPTIX is piloting its solutions in seven distinct European cities, each representing unique challenges and opportunities:

  • Denmark: Smart integration of district heating and cooling systems.
  • Sweden: Renewable energy optimization in urban districts.
  • Germany: Advanced energy storage and management solutions.
  • Turkey: Resilient and scalable PED frameworks for warmer climates.
  • Austria: Community-driven energy innovations.
  • Romania: Exploring digitalization in low-energy districts.
  • Ireland: Cutting-edge blockchain applications for energy trading.

Work Packages

OPTIX is structured into several key work packages to ensure effective project implementation:

  • WP1: Project Coordination and Management
    • Ensure efficient communication among partners.
    • Monitor project milestones and deliverables.
  • WP2: Technology Integration for PEDs
    • Develop advanced solar PV and storage solutions.
    • Optimize heating and cooling systems.
  • WP3: Digital Twin Development
    • Create dynamic digital models for real-time energy management.
    • Integrate simulation tools with monitoring platforms.
  • WP4: Energy Trading and Blockchain Solutions
    • Implement secure peer-to-peer energy trading platforms.
    • Facilitate community energy markets.
  • WP5: Pilot Demonstrations
    • Test and validate solutions in diverse urban environments.
    • Document lessons learned for scalability.
  • WP6: Knowledge Dissemination
    • Share findings with stakeholders and policymakers.
    • Engage with the broader energy research community.
